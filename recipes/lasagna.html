Skillet Lasagna

Description

This no-bake skillet lasagna is made right on your stovetop and is a fast and easy alternative to store-bought hamburger mixes!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 can diced onions
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 teaspons basil
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups noodles
  • 1 cup shredded mozarella cheese

Steps

  1. In skillet, cook beef and drain grease.
  2. Add in sauce and seasonings. Cook over low heat about 15 minutes. Until sauce is hot.
  3. Meanwhile, bring water to boil and cook noodles about 8 minutes. Drain water from noodles.
  4. Combine sauce and noodles, add cheese on top.
  5. Place skillet under hot broiler. Cook till cheese is gold and bubbly. About 3 to 5 minutes.
